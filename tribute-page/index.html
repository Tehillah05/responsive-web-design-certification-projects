About Dr. Zimba

Dr. Moffat Zimba is a visionary academic, pastor, and leader who co-founded Northrise University in Ndola, Zambia, in 2004 with his wife, Doreen, aiming to provide affordable, high-quality, Christian-based education to empower Zambians.

Dr. Zimba, a Zambian native with a doctorate in theology and leadership, has devoted his life to holistic student development. Under his leadership, Northrise University has become a leading private institution in Zambia, celebrated for academic excellence, community service, and faith-based education.

Dr. Zimba's legacy lies in empowering thousands of graduates who excel across various sectors, exemplifying his servant leadership, resilience, and dedication to building a brighter future for Zambia.
